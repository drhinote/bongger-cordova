Bongger

The most experimental crytocurrency in the world!

Welcome to Bongger :) Bongger was first released in April of 2014 (around 4/20, heh...). Since then, it has been regarded as an underdog, but it has remained as stable and useful as it's progenetors: Bitcoin & Litecoin. Bongger is an deflationary currency that will over time continue to increase in value as there is only a limited supply of the coin. The total coin supply is set at 504,000,000. The first 84,000,000 were reserved for promotions & payment for development work. The remaining 420,000,000 coins are currently being generated as subsidies for the miners that process the transactions sent to and from the users of Bongger.
